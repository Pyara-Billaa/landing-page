Mugiwara no ichimi

Mugiwara no Ichimi, also known as the Straw Hat Pirates, is the main pirate crew in the anime and manga series One Piece. Led by Monkey D. Luffy, the crew's name comes from Luffy's signature straw hat, which was a gift from his friend Shanks. The crew initially sailed on the Going Merry, later acquiring the Thousand Sunny. The Straw Hats are known for their loyalty, adventurous spirit, and their combined goal of finding the legendary treasure, One Piece.
